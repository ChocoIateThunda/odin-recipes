ButterMilk Pancakes Recipe

Description

Perfect buermilk pancake recipe for sunday breakfast!
This easy to make recipe will produce pancaks so light and fluffy itll be like eating a cloud!

Ingredients

  • 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 ¼ cups buttermilk
  • 1 ¼ cups buttermilk
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda

Directions

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Combine flour, buttermilk, sugar, oil, egg, baking powder, and baking soda in a blender. Purée until smooth.
  3. Pour batter onto the hot skillet to form 5 pancakes. Flip pancakes when edges appear to harden.
    Flip pancakes; cook until the bottoms are golden brown.
